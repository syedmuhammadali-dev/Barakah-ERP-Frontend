# Barakah ERP: Product Requirements & Screen Map

## Product Vision

Barakah ERP is a premium SaaS retail management system designed for global shop owners. It uniquely integrates standard ERP features (Inventory, Sales, CRM) with Islamic Business Compliance (Zakat calculation, Amanat tracking, and Shariah-compliant business purification).

## Visual Direction

- **Style**: "Stripe meets Linear" — high-density but clean, deep shadows, subtle gradients, and rounded corners (12px-16px).

- **Palette**:

  - Primary: Emerald Green (Growth/Islamic)

  - Accents: Metallic Gold (Premium/Value)

  - Neutrals: Deep Slate/Gray (Enterprise stability)

  - Background: Crisp White (Light) / Rich Navy-Black (Dark)
- **Typography**: Inter or Geist for a technical, modern feel.


## Proposed Screen List

### Phase 1: Public & Entry

1. **Landing Page**: Marketing site with hero, features, pricing, and FAQ.

2. **Auth Suite (Combined)**: Login, Register, Forgot Password, and OTP.


### Phase 2: Core Dashboard & Inventory

3. **Main Dashboard**: The "Command Center" with 9 KPIs, sales trends, and alerts.

4. **Universal Inventory**: Advanced table with Islamic stock fields (Amanat).

5. **Company Returns**: Tracking supplier-side returns and refunds.


### Phase 3: Sales & Finance

6. **Sales Management**: Transaction history and recording.

7. **Salesman Ledger**: Individual salesman performance and account tracking.

8. **Demand / Restock**: Kanban board for procurement planning.


### Phase 4: Islamic Compliance & Reporting

9. **Islamic Compliance Center**: Zakat calculator and purification alerts.

10. **Reports Center**: Holistic business intelligence exports.

11. **Billing & Invoices**: High-fidelity PDF/Print-ready invoice template.


### Phase 5: Administration

12. **Notification Center**: System-wide alerts and reminders.

13. **Settings**: Shop configuration and Islamic Mode toggle.

14. **Audit Logs**: Enterprise security and action tracking.


## Key Design Patterns

- **Sidebar Navigation**: Persistent left rail with grouped modules.

- **Global Search**: Command-K style interface.

- **Action Headers**: Standardized breadcrumbs + primary action (Add/Export).

- **Empty States**: Custom illustrations for new accounts.
